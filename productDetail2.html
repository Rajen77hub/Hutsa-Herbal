Key Ingredients

14 Active Botanical Oils

An exceptionally nourishing blend of low comedegenic active oils -- Sacha Inchi, Prickly Pear, Cacay, Rosehip, Rock Rose, Juniper, Jojoba, Vetiver, Cedar, Squalane, Babassu, Linseed, Murumuru & Frankincense -- rich in vitamins A, E & F and an optimized ratio of Omegas 3, 6 & 9 to repair, regenerate, brighten, and boost without causing breakouts.

Amber Extract

A potent source of skin brightening Succinic Acid, this golden nectar of fossilized tree resin infused with antioxidants, is revered for its healing, anti-inflammatory properties. 

Spanish Needle, Marshmallow & Licorice Root Extracts

Natural retinol and two powerful skin brightening extracts bring fresh skin cells to the surface, reduce visible signs of premature aging including fine lines, and confer an aura of protection against environmental stressors, including UV damage.

Copaiba Oil

With strong anti-inflammatory properties from its definitive amounts of Î²-Caryophyllene, this organic oleoresin has been used to fight blemishes and calm irritated skin.

How To Use

Massage 3 to 8 drops once or twice daily into cleansed skin.

Use after serum or moisturizer.

To use as a dark spot corrector apply 1 to 2 drops to targeted areas.

For dry environments, blend with Taos Blue Day Cream to help seal in added moisture.

Gently shake before use.
